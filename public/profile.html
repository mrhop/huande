COMPANY PROFILE

Incorporated in Beijing in 2005, Huan De Group is an experienced industrial future cities developer which aims to strengthen the cooperation and relationship between Europe and China. Huan De Group makes a significant contribution to the understanding of how the past shapes the future.

Huan De Group is governed by four key principles in the development of new industrial future cities, which include:

  • A commitment to green development
  • A commitment to quality of life in our cities
  • A commitment to continuously innovate
  • A commitment to bring industries together

“The rapid growth in China has produced an alienation between the environment and the human being. It requires an intelligent urbanization. The Human Being has to be reconciled with its environment. People in harmony with their environment can again consume and produce ” Florian von Tucher, Chairman of Tucher Group and EUTECC

  • Huan De Group is an experienced industrial future cities developer and a multinational enterprise with German root, rich development and investment experience.

  • The strength behind the growth of Huan De Group is its people. It possesses an organizational system of professional management teams and business units.

  • Huan De Group seeks out opportunities with the Made in China 2025 Plan that aims to improve production efficiency and quality.
