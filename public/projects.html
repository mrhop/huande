Huan De Future City projects (short of HD FC projects) are to be developed by Huan De Group, the developing arm of Tucher Group, with the target to showcase sustainable urbanization and create "Low-carbon and Green City" project featured by innovative future industries.

HD FC projects are dedicated to introduce European innovation leaders into the Chinese market and create a platform for the exchanges on high-tech products and technologies between the European and Chinese companies, which in turn help the industrial upgrade of local cities and facilitate the transformation of the local area from equipment importation to local production.

The projects will be built into a European industrial parks centering on the elements of low carbon city and environment protection. Besides, the HD FC projects will not only provide landing properties and services for the entering industries but will also be equipped with European style commercial components and eco homes focused on 3 core elements of Sustainable Urbanization, Life Science and Industry 4.0.

HD FC projects also prioritizes energy efficiency over the entire life cycle of buildings and project operation as sustainable architecture; minimizes the negative environmental impact by efficiency and moderation in the use of materials, energy, development space and the ecosystem and uses a conscious approach to energy and ecological conservation in the design and operation. In addition, HD FC projects intend to provide the natural resources and ecosystem services upon which the economy and society depend without undermining the integrity and stability of the natural systems. By consistently contributing to the local community and society with sustainable development, HD FC projects support, facilitate and monitor the use of renewable energy and re-used products and introduce advanced environmental protection technology and equipment.
