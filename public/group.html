TUCHER GROUP

Tucher Group is the holding company of the businesses of the Tucher Family, around Thomas von Tucher and his son Florian von Tucher. The Tucher are an old and traditional German family and were first mentioned in 1309. During 14th and 15th century the Tucher Family made its fortune by trading from the central city of Nuremberg to the rest of Europe, especially Venice, which was the European end of the Silk road at that time.Tucher Group has 3 main pillars of businesses, that is, Project Development (Huan De Group), Investment (Euro Sino Invest Group) and Engineering (M&P Group) and it’s the shareholder and partner of Huan De Group.

Huan De Group is the Chinese development arm of the Tucher Group and aims to bring the Tucher Family’s values and traditions to our Chinese projects and partners. It is now the largest European real estate developer in China.

Mr. Florian von Tucher who is a civil engineer with extensive China experience. He started the China business for the Tucher Group in 2005 and managed it before being promoted to be the Chairman of Tucher Group in 2015.

In 2015, Mr. Florian von Tucher came back from China to Europe and devoted himself into bridging the European companies to China. As the chairman of Tucher Group and European Technology Chamber (EUTECC), he created a special mode to bring European Innovation leaders to China.
